Think Like an Amateur, Do As an Expert

这是Prof.Takeo Kanade在2018 Embedding VIsion Summit上的keynote的takeaway。

-What is Good Research?

  • Good science responds to real phenomena or real problems

  • Good science is in the details

  • Good science makes a difference

-To conduct useful and fun research:

  • Start with a scenario:Picture the success-what can happen;how and where will it be used?
  • Think freely with fun
  • The story expands,and you talk about it
  • Other people can join

Think like an amateur,do as an expert--when you are starting to solve a problem,think like a novice,an amateur by consciously avoiding perconveived biases.When implementing a solution,adpot a meticulous and thorough approach.

-Value of Reseach

  • Research dose not have to be new.

  • "Newness itself is not virtue,usefulness is"

What is Research and How to do it?

这是UCB马毅老师2009做的报告

  • Research is not a job,and is more like a hobby

  • All you need is passion...

  • Research is about pushing the limits:Dismiss the problem is the solution is not interesting enough.

  • Research is a game for which you get to set the rules,but is cannot be a trivial one

  • A good engineer needs not to be a good researcher;a good researcher needs not to be a good engineer.

    1)Engineering is about optimization,research is to find the limits and better ways for optimization

    2)Engineering is about invention,research is to explain why an invention works.

    3)Engineering research is not science nor empirical science,it is close to applied math or applied sciences.

  • Different levels of research

    1)level one:research assistant

    Learn the drill

    2)level two:homework like research

    build up your confidence

    3)Level three:formulating your own problem and solve it

    exercise your creativity

    4)level four:formulating problems for others to solve

    realize your vision

  • Knowing what to do is more important than knowing how.

  • What is good research?

    1)Novely(better if you were not to able to publish is at first)

    2)Simplicity(better if your advisor refused to grant you a degree)

    3)Universality（better if others found it trivial at first sight）

  • You recognize a great research result if it keeps you up all night,feeling like your first date

  • Warning Signs of Bogus Research

    1)Prove by successful instances

    2)Compound and conquer

    3)Results too complicated to reproduce

    4)Reinvent the wheel without proper acknowledgment

    5)Tackle ill-posed problems directly

    6)Solicit popularity over expert opinions

    7)Ocam's razor reversed

    8)Monkey collects corn cobs

    Such results will not stand the test of time

  • How to do research?Common misunderstanding

    1)Think research is only for genius

    2)Think yourself is a genius

    3)Think you need to know everything about the subject in advance

    4)Think you should wait for the most important problem to work on

    5)Think a solution is correct just because you cannot find anything wrong

  • How to do research?

    1)Be both confident and humble

    2)Be both critical and collaborative

    3)Be both ambitious and realistic

    4)Be proactive and willing to take a chance

    The only way you can improve yourself is to learn thing that you are the most afraid of!

  • How dose doing research feel like?

    1)An endless cycle between excitement and depression...

    2)An endless cycle between sense of success and failure...

    3)An endless cycle between over-confidenrce and self-doubt...

    Reseach needs faith in there are always interesting new things for you to discover and by doing so you can make the world a better place!
